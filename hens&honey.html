Hens & Honey

The goal of this project was to redesign Hens & Honey small business website to better support the user experience while maintaining their current style. One problem I wanted to solve was communicating which location provided which services and make sure all pages were linked in one cohesive website.

My role and responsibilites

For this project I designed and developed the front end of the website. I was responsible for research, interviews, design, and development.

This project was in collaboration with Hens & Honey to design a better user experience for all. Some problems I immediately addressed in their original website was a lack of links to their web-pages, and the confusing nature of the page layouts. For example, their business has two locations, and this was poorly organized on their homepage. I designed a user persona to better relate personal experiences to what users might be looking for in a website.

I created wire-frames to visualize where this information might go.

Next, I built the wire-frames in Figma to better understand how these structures might appear on a digital screen.

Now, I was ready for refining the design and creating my high fidelity prototypes. These designs were developed for desktop, tablet, and smartphone screens. For a more detailed look, here is the link to my Figma file.
