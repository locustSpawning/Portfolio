About

Hi, I'm Chelsea, a self taught designer who loves solving problems, collaboration, and using my design skills to turn a vision into a reality.

I have a passion for learning, which led me to pursue a career in tech, while my creativity pushed be towards UX/UI design.

Values

As a homesteader, I value doing things right - meaning ensuring things are functional, logical, and built to last. I care about fixing things now so they don't break later. Creating a strong foundation provides a smooth operation for the future, and this view carries over to coding and design as well.

I believe that creating things with the intention of functionality and user-friendliness is essential for a successful product and something that I can be proud of being a part of designing.

If you feel the same, please consider reaching out!
