Rosie’s Farm Market

TThe goal of this project was to update a current website to meet modern day standards while improving accessibility for all users. Additionally, I wanted to make this website dynamic and better suited for mobile screens.

My role and responsibilites

For this project I designed and developed the front end of this website. I was responsible for research, interviews, design, and development.

This project, I collaborated with Rosie’s to create a more modern website that better reflects their business and the products they offer. One of my main priorities for this redesign was to create a mobile friendly version of their website, since most users will likely be clicking on their website through Google Maps and while on the go!
I started with some paper wire-frames to gain an understanding of how the information architecture might be best designed.

Next, I designed the High Fidelity prototypes in Figma. I focused on both desktop and mobile in these mock ups. For a more detailed look, here is the link to my Figma file.

Mobile

The final steps involved coding the website and communicating with Lisa, one of the business owners, for deploying the website to the public!
